FOR ATHLETES WHO RUN TO TRAIN.

The Jordan Air Zoom Renegade is a lightweight, stable shoe designed to help athletes who run to train get the most from their momentum. It features twin cushioning units that are widely spaced for stability. A thin, contoured plate offers support.

Explosive Response

Responsive Zoom Air units under the ball of your foot extend out from the sides, helping to keep you stable. Cushlon foam adds to the sensation and helps make smooth, comfortable heel-to-toe transitions.

Extra Pop

A thin, firm plate running from toe to heel works with the Zoom Air for support. The plate is higher in front.

Light and Ventilated

The nano-printed material through the upper and tongue is thin, lightweight and breathable.

Smooth Transitions

A flexible rubber band connects the front and back parts of the outsole to help smooth the sensation when rolling from your heel onto your toes.
